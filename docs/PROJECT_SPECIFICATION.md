# Open Now Platform

Version: 1.0

---

## Project Vision

Open Now Platform is a real-time business discovery platform.

The platform is NOT a traditional business directory.

Instead, it helps users discover businesses that are OPEN RIGHT NOW based on confirmation from business owners.

Business owners manually confirm their business is open by pressing a button inside their dashboard.

Each confirmation remains valid for 12 hours.

This makes the platform different from Google Maps because information comes directly from business owners in real time.

---

## Main Goal

Help people quickly find open essential services nearby.

## Business Status Logic

Business owners can press:

OPEN NOW

or

CLOSED

If OPEN NOW is pressed:

Status becomes active for exactly 12 hours.

After 12 hours the business is automatically considered closed until confirmed again.

The platform only promotes businesses confirmed recently.
